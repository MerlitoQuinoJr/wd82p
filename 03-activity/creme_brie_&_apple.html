ASPARAGUS & CHEESE

INGREDIENT STATEMENT:

Chicken Breast With Rib Meat (Containing Up To 18% Of A Solution Of Water, Potato Starch, Salt, Modified Food Starch, Yeast Extract, Chicken Broth, Maltodextrin,Dextrose, Sugar, Natural Flavors, Spices, Chicken Fat, Corn Syrup Solids, Citric Acid, Hydrolyzed Corn Gluten, Concentrated Lemon Juice), Asparagus, Pasteurized Process Swiss And American Cheese (Swiss Cheese [Cultured Milk And Skim Milk, Salt, Enzymes],American Cheese [Cultured Milk And Skim Milk, Salt, Enzymes], Cream, Sodium Phosphate, Salt), Enriched Wheat Flour (Niacin, Reduced Iron, Thiamine Mononitrate, Riboflavin, Folic Acid), Water, Vegetable Oil, Contains 2% Or Less Of: Modified Food Starch, Salt, Whole Wheat Flour, Sugar, Yeast, Spices, Leavening (Sodium Acid Pyrophosphate, Sodium Bicarbonate), Dextrose, Yeast Extract, Natural Flavors, Sodium Alginate, Yellow Corn Flour, Dehydrated Parsley, Extractives Of Turmeric, Paprika, Annatto And Celery Seed. Prebrowned In Vegetable Oil. CONTAINS: MILK, WHEAT

Conventional Oven

Preheat oven to 375 F. Remove frozen raw breast(s) from pouch(es) and place on metal baking sheet, place each breast 2 inches apart. Bake 1-2 pieces for 34 to 36 minutes and 3-6 pieces for 37 to 40 minutes. Caution: Filling will be hot andmay splatter, allow to cool (for) 3 minutes before serving.
