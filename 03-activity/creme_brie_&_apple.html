Prebrowned In Vegetable Oil. CONTAINS: MILK, WHEAT

Conventional Oven

Preheat oven to 375 F. Remove frozen raw breast(s) from pouch(es) and place on metal baking sheet, place each breast 2 inches apart. Bake 1-2 pieces for 31 to 34 minutes and 3-6 pieces for 36 to 39 minutes. Caution: Filling will be hot andmay splatter, allow to cool (for) 3 minutes before serving.
