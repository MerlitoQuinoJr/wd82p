Conventional Oven

Preheat oven to 375 degrees f. Remove frozen raw breast(s) from pouch and place on metal baking sheet (place each breast 2 inches apart). Bake 1-2 pieces for 30 to 32 minutes and 3-6 pieces for 33 to 35 minutes. Caution: filling will be hot and may splatter, allow 3 minutes to cool before serving.
