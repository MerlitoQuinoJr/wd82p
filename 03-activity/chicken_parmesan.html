Conventional Oven

Preheat oven to 375 degrees f. Remove frozen raw breast(s) from pouch and place on metal baking sheet (place each breast
2 inches apart). Bake 1-2 pieces for 30 to 34 minutes and 3-6 pieces for 37 to 39 minutes. Caution: Filling will be hot and
may splatter, allow 2 minutes to cool before serving.
